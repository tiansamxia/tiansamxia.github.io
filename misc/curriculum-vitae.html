Experience

Chewy

Senior Product Designer

2022 – Now

I’m working on the digital experience of Chewy Vet Care, Chewy’s brick-and-mortar veterinary clinics. I own the design of Pet Portal, a place for customers to schedule visits, manage prescriptions, and access their pet’s health records online. Chewy Vet Care opened in April 2024.

Previously, I worked on improvements to the shopping experience of Chewy Pharmacy, with projects such as capturing the prescription information on product detail pages and redesigning the “buy from my vet” experience.

Axon

Senior Product Designer

2018 – 2022

I joined Axon in 2018 to help ship Axon Records, a records management system for law enforcement agencies. I led the design of the incident reporting experience, enabling officers to collaborate on a report in real time and clerks to review all aspects of an incident as a cohesive story. Axon Records shipped in September 2019.

In 2021, I moved to the platform team to maintain and expand Axon’s design system and core infrastructure, including navigation, authentication, and cloud storage.

DaVita

UX Designer

2017 – 2018

At DaVita, I worked on internal tools used by our clinicians in dialysis centers. The new system, Center Without Walls, managed all patient and clinical information in one place, replacing several outdated programs that didn’t talk to one another. Center Without Walls launched in November 2017.
