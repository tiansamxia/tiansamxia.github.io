About

I’m a product designer experienced in interaction design, information architecture, and design systems. I view myself as a Gestaltingenieur who turns complex, ambiguous, tricky problems into clear, thoughtful, elegant solutions for the user. Currently I work at Axon in Seattle.

In my own time, I carry my camera wherever I go and attempt to save the decisive moments happening before my eyes. I also enjoy nerdy conversations about cameras, lenses, and my “desert island kit.”
