Tian (Sam) Xia

Interaction Designer

Seattle, Washington

Hello, I’m Sam. I am an interaction designer living in Seattle, Washington. I currently work at Chewy.

Over the past 10 years, I have designed and shipped products that helped people explore their genes, treat dialysis patients, solve crimes, and care for their pets. I’m known for thinking systematically, diving deep, and having a great eye for detail.

Besides design, I’m an avid photographer, car enthusiast, and aviation fan. I can talk days about Hasselblads, Leicas, and Porsches. If you share a passion for cameras, cars, aircrafts, and other beautifully made objects, let’s connect.
